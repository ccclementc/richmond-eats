🥢 食乜好？使用說明

What to Eat? — User Guide

呢個App做乜？What is this app?

🍚
幫你揀今日去邊度食飯，仲話你知每間嘢食乜最好食。全部係列治文嘅餐廳。
It picks a Richmond restaurant for you and shows each place's best dishes.

日常用法（三步）Everyday use — 3 steps

🎲
1. 撳紅色大掣「幫我揀！」，App就會揀一間啱依家時段嘅餐廳。
1. Tap the big red "Pick For Me!" button — it chooses a restaurant that suits the time of day.
🔄
2. 唔啱心水？撳黃色「換一間」，即刻換過第二間。
2. Don't fancy it? Tap "Pick Again" for a different one.
🍽️
3. 睇「推介菜式」，有👍招牌就係最好食嗰啲。
3. Check "Recommended Dishes" — 👍 marks the signatures.
💡 想收窄範圍：先撳上面嘅掣揀時段、菜式（可以揀幾樣）、地區同價錢，再撳「幫我揀」。撳「全部」就係唔限。
To narrow it down, tap the time / cuisine / area / price buttons first. "All" means no limit.

餐廳頁面嘅掣Buttons on a restaurant page

📞
打電話 — 一撳就直接打去餐廳（訂枱或者叫外賣）。
Call — phones the restaurant directly (reserve or order takeout).
🗺️
地圖 — 開Google地圖，睇地址、營業時間、點去。
Map — opens Google Maps with address, hours and directions.
📋
搵菜單 — 幫你上網搵呢間餐廳嘅餐牌。
Menu — searches the web for the restaurant's menu.
📤
話俾屋企人 — 將間餐廳傳去WhatsApp／微信，同屋企人商量。
Share — sends the pick to WhatsApp/WeChat for the family.

📝 落單紙（去到餐廳用）Order Card — use at the restaurant

1️⃣
撳「落單紙」，App已經幫你揀定招牌菜同你鍾意嘅菜。
Tap "Order Card" — signatures and your ❤️ dishes are pre-selected.
2️⃣
撳一下加減菜式，用＋／−較數量。
Tap dishes to add or remove; use ＋/− for quantity.
3️⃣
撳「顯示俾侍應睇」，出大字版，直接俾侍應睇就落到單。
Tap "Show to Server" — big-text version to show the server.

記住你嘅口味Remembering your tastes

⭐
最愛 — 撳星星記低鍾意嘅餐廳，App會多啲揀佢哋。撳「只睇最愛」淨係睇老地方。
Star favourite restaurants — the picker favours them. "Favourites Only" shows just your regulars.
❤️
鍾意嘅菜 — 撳心心記低，落單紙會自動幫你揀埋。
Heart dishes you love — the order card pre-selects them.
✅
食咗 — 食完撳一下，App三日內唔會再推薦同一間，等你唔會日日食同一樣嘢。
"Ate Here" — tap after a meal; the picker skips that place for 3 days so you get variety.
🚫
唔要呢間 — 唔啱口味嘅餐廳，以後唔會再出現。
Hide — restaurants you never want suggested again.

屋企人專區For family members

＋
加餐廳／加菜式 — 發現新餐廳或者好菜，隨時加入。加菜式可以寫低邊個推介（例如「阿女推介」）。
Add restaurants or dishes any time. Dishes can carry a recommender name, e.g. "阿女推介".
✏️
修改 — 資料有錯（電話、地址、執咗笠）就撳修改更正。
Edit — fix wrong details or closed restaurants.
⬇️
備份／還原 — 換新電話時，先「備份」，喺新機「還原」，星星心心全部帶走。
Backup / Restore — moves favourites and hearts to a new phone.
⚠️ 每部電話嘅星星、心心係各自儲存嘅，唔會自動同步。要改所有人見到嘅餐廳名單，請改GitHub上面嘅 restaurants.json（睇 GITHUB_SETUP.md）。
Stars/hearts live on each phone separately. To change the shared restaurant list for everyone, edit restaurants.json on GitHub (see GITHUB_SETUP.md).

裝喺手機面（好似App咁）Put it on the home screen

📱
iPhone：用Safari開網址 → 撳分享掣（□↑）→「加至主畫面」
Android：用Chrome開 → 撳右上⋮ →「新增至主畫面」
iPhone: Safari → Share (□↑) → "Add to Home Screen". Android: Chrome → ⋮ → "Add to Home screen".
